About Website


Some more useful informations

At first, I made this website because when my friends found out about my Github they were lost trying to find the exact thing they needed, so I made a small web-page they could download that would repertory every projects I made with every links, wikis, images they needed. One of my friend told me that I should make a website instead because it would be easier for me to send a link than a page, but also because if someone new wants to see my work, they don't have to ask me for the file. I then started working on a first main page on the 17th July 2020 a whole night and came up with a bad design, why? Because I knew all I needed about html, but knew nothing about CSS. I directly downloaded a Html & CSS book named "HTML&CSS design and build websites by Duckett" which I had pleasure to read during 2 whole weeks, at the end I knew the few I had to know to start coding a working website in html and CSS. And voila, after 6 days of hard work the first page of the website was done, it was published on the 19th August 2020. I had my inspirations from two friends of mine who helped me a lot on debugging my code and optimizing it. And now here it is, TheNolle's website!
